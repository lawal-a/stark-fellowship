About the Bootcamp

Building for the Kingdom

The STARK Entrepreneurship Bootcamp equips faith-driven founders to birth and grow kingdom-relevant businesses — guided by seasoned ministers across three days of teaching, panels, and breakout sessions. STARK stands for The Stars of God's Ark. This is the Second Edition, coming to both Jos and Benue State simultaneously in July 2026.
