About STARK

What is STARK Fellowship?

STARK is a fellowship of entrepreneurs in service to God through a love-driven business approach. We exist to teach a Love-Based Entrepreneurship Model — helping brethren start right or recalibrate businesses to please God.

Knowledge-capital platform — rigorous curriculum grounded in Kingdom principles and practical business wisdom.

Community-first — Graduates become Stark Fellows embedded in a lifelong network of mission-minded entrepreneurs.

State leadership — Select Stark Fellows become State Ambassadors — Stark Leaders — extending the vision nationwide.

✦

Programs

The STARK Curriculum

Five distinct tracks — each designed for a specific stage and type of entrepreneur. Every journey starts at The Foundation.

LBI

Life Business Incubator

For product-based, tech-driven, and scalable startups. The flagship incubator experience that equips founders to build products God can bless.

SOT

School of Timothy

The co-founders academy. Grounded in the Paul-Timothy model — impartation, mentorship, and intentional formation for entrepreneurial teams.

SME School

SME School

For services-based businesses in the real economy. Practical, accessible Kingdom stewardship principles for trades, agencies, and local enterprises.

Growth Mindset

Growth Mindset

For growth-stage entrepreneurs repositioning their business to please God. Deep recalibration of vision, culture, strategy, and mission alignment.

The Foundation

The Foundation

A compendium of foundational messages on stewardship, purpose, and Kingdom business from Pastor Emeka. Compulsory with every other program — and available as a standalone track for those who want to begin their Kingdom-business journey.

⭐ Included with all programs  ·  Can stand alone
