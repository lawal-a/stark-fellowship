About STARK

Team101:
Brethren in Business.

STARK Fellowship is an expression of Team101, a covenant community of brethren in business. Supported by Aidi, Forfond, and Heave Ventures, we exist to raise Kingdom entrepreneurs who build businesses that serve God, not just the market.

Brethren in Business, a covenant community of Kingdom entrepreneurs building together, sharpening one another, holding each other to a higher standard.

Supported by Aidi, Forfond & Heave Ventures, the institutional backbone giving STARK the resources to go deeper and wider.

State Ambassadors, select STARK Fellows become Stark Leaders, extending the vision state by state across Nigeria and beyond.

Philosophy

The Master's Business.

Four principles that define Kingdom entrepreneurship, the foundation beneath every STARK program.

Select a principle to explore
01 / ACCOLADES

God Gives
the Accolades.

The standard is not Forbes, it is "Well done, good and faithful servant." Success in The Master's Business is defined by faithfulness, not fame. You build to hear those words.

"Well done, good and faithful servant… enter into the joy of your lord." Matthew 25:23

Accolades
02 / LEADING

Trust God's
Leadings.

In business, the most critical decisions are often too complex for pure analysis. Train your spirit to hear God accurately, strategy, timing, partnerships, all led by the Spirit.

"For as many as are led by the Spirit of God, these are sons of God." Romans 8:14

Leading
03 / SERVICE

Serve
Others.

Everything that truly lives does not live for itself. A business that exists purely for profit is already dying. Kingdom businesses exist to serve, customers, communities, and creation.

"Whoever wants to become great among you must be your servant." Matthew 20:26

Service
04 / GROWTH

Grow
Sustainably.

Purpose-driven, not just profit-driven. Sustainable growth is rooted in a clear why, not market pressure. The Master's Business grows with integrity, serving the long game of God's Kingdom.

"Commit your work to the Lord, and your plans will be established." Proverbs 16:3

Growth
